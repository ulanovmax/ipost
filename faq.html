FAQ

The iPost Main Screen

The main window in iPost lets you add, remove and re-order the contents of an envelope; check the address will be visible and correct any issues; view the content of the envelope; and submit it to be printed and posted.

  1. Envelope Id

    This is a unique Id that lets you identify the envelope if you ever need to ask for help from your administrator or IT support

  2. Envelope Title

    You can type in a title to help you identify the envelope – e.g. “Letter to Mr. Smith Ref 123/ABC/456”

  3. Postage Type

    How the envelope will be posted. You can change this if your adminstrator has given you permission

  4. Delete Envelope

    Clicking the X will delete the entire envelope (it will not delete the original files from your computer)

  5. Menu

    • Help – View the Help pages
    • My Account – Options to change your password or log out
    • My Envelopes – View your recently submitted envelopes

  6. Physical Inserts

    View any physical inserts you have access to; you can then drag and drop these onto the “Add to Envelope” region (12, below)

  7. Address Window

    This area shows the part of your document that will be visible when printed and placed in a physical envelope. You can click this window to add an address if needed.

  8. Submit Envelope

    When you’re happy with the envelope, click this button to sbumit it to be printed and posted

  9. May Fold indicator

    By default, your documents may be folded into a normal (non-A4) envelope. If your administrator has given you permission, you can untick this box to ensure the document is not folded, and is posted in an A4 envelope

  10. Number of Items

    An indicated to show how many documents you have added to the envelope

  11. The Document Window

    Shows the documents in your envelope, in the order they will be printed. Click to View any document and change it spaper type. Drag and drop documents to change their order

  12. Add to Envelope region

    Click this region to choose additional documents to add to the envelope, or drag and drop them from Windows Explorer or your desktop

  13. Remove Document

    Click the small X to remove any document from the envelope. This will not delete the original file from your computer

What File Formats Can I Use?

iPost supports documents in the following formats:

  • Microsoft Word and related formats including .DOC, .DOCX, .RTF, .TXT, .XPS, .OOXML, .ODT, FlatOpc, .XML, .HTML, .XHTML

  • Adobe PDF

  • Microsoft Excel and related formats including .XLS, .XLSX, .CSV, .XLSM, .XLSB, .ODS, .XLTX, .XLTM, SpreadsheetML, Tab-delimited

  • Microsoft Powerpoint and related formats including .PPT, .PPTX, .PPS, .POT, .POTX, .PPSX, .ODP, PresentationML

  • All documents must be A4 (portrait or landscape)

My Documents Are in the Wrong Order

You can change the order of the documents by dragging and dropping them in the Document Viewer to the right of your envelope. The order they appear in is the order they’ll be printed in.

Note that if you’ve added an address to your envelope, it will always appear in the first position, and that physical inserts will always appear at the end.

The address isn't visible on my envelope

iPost’s address window shows the area of your document that will be visible when it is printed, folded and placed into an envelope.

If the address doesn’t appear in the correct place, you could edit your document to move the address to the correct place. An easier option is to add an address within iPost.

To do this, click on the address window and a new screen will pop up. Enter the recipient’s name and address (searching by Postcode or Organisation if you wish) and click to add the address.

How do I change the paper type or print double-sided?

Your print administrators will have set up a default paper type. To change this, or to specify whether pages should be printed single-or double-sided, click to View the document in question. You will see a popup window that lets you choose from the options your print administrator has set up.
