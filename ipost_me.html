Who is iPost Me for?

With over a billion letters delivered in the UK each month, posting correspondence is still a crucial part of the way we communicate. Whether you are an individual sending 1 item of mail a year or a micro business, looking for more professional looking mail pieces forget buying stamps!

iPost is a quick and easy, no hassle, pay-as-you-go solution for you.

No contract. No templates. No problems

iPost allows you to send any file format in the post from any device on a simple pay-as-you-go basis. Simply set up an account with as little as £1 credit and you’re good to go.

Safe. Secure. Seamless

Sending sensitive material with iPost is as safe, if not safer, than through traditional means. Documents are printed and enveloped at a dedicated UKMail fulfilment centre, which complies with all security and data protection regulations.

What kind of files can iPost send?

iPost supports virtually every file type you could want to send in the mail from any hard drive or cloud platform.

Mail made easy

Whether you’re corresponding informally with friends or sending business letters, device to delivery only takes a few clicks with iPost.
