We are iComunico Software:

specialists in template-free hybrid mailing for enterprise-level organisations

We are a dynamic innovative software business developing solutions for enterprise-grade businesses. Our vast experience in document management and output within large organisations and our understanding of the challenges facing them, has led us to the development of iPost®

By enabling companies to process their large volumes of ad-hoc outgoing documents through the same process as their system generated bulk mail, we are able to deliver savings of up to 50% on postal costs alone.

iPost® also removes the need for on-site hardware typically used for ad-hoc mail, such as local printers and franking machines, as well as wasted time and effort from staff.

Make your hybrid mailing faster, more flexible and more cost effective with iPost® today.
