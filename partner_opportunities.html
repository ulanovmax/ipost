Partner Opportunities

iPost Business is provided by our network of partners. Becoming an approved partner allows you to build a recurring revenue stream whilst providing a superb state of the art application for your customers.

Our offer has been designed from the outset to offer the customer best value with no hidden or unfair terms. No Contracts no set up fees, much better than their current method be that franking, PPI license or stamps. Even If the potential customer acquired a new franking machine yesterday, they are still better off switching it off canceling the maintenance contract and moving to iPost. If they are currently using another traditional outsourcer or mailing house that will involve printer driver downloads templates and other IT problems. iPost takes the documents as they are. It’s a true web app and the output is printed inside the mail distribution centre itself cutting out completely the collection process. iPost is not a static product exciting releases and new functionality are on the way.

Whether you are in the mailing business already, or provide IT solutions to business or the private sector iPost gives you a state of the art SAAS product without any objections or barriers.
